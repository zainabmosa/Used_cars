# Understanding Used Car Prices
### An Exploratory Data Analysis of Used Car Listings in the United States

## Project Overview

This project explores a dataset of 426,880 used car listings collected from Craigslist across the United States.

The main goal of this project is to understand the factors that affect used car prices and help buyers make better purchasing decisions by comparing similar vehicles.

The analysis focuses on how different vehicle characteristics influence price, including manufacturing year, mileage, condition, manufacturer, transmission, vehicle size, and vehicle type.

---

## Problem Statement

With 426,880 used car listings across the United States, buyers have many different options with different prices and specifications. This makes it difficult to understand what affects used car prices and to compare similar cars to determine a fair market price. As a result, buyers may make poor purchasing decisions.

---

## Objectives

1. How can two cars from the same manufacturer and year have different prices?

2. How can two cars from the same manufacturer, model, and year have different prices?

3. How does odometer mileage affect vehicle prices?

4. How do used car prices vary across different vehicle conditions?

5. How do used car prices vary across different vehicle types?

6. How does the manufacturing year affect vehicle prices?

7. How do used car prices vary across different manufacturers?

## Key Findings

- Even cars from the same manufacturer and year can have different prices because of the model.

- Even cars from the same manufacturer, model, and year can have different prices because of factors such as mileage, condition, transmission, and vehicle type.

- Odometer mileage has a strong effect on price. Cars with lower mileage generally have higher prices.

- Used car prices vary depending on the vehicle condition, and cars in better condition generally have higher prices.

- Vehicle type also has a noticeable effect on used car prices, Especially full size.

- Prices show a slight increase for very old vehicles, remain lower for some middle-year models, then increase significantly for newer vehicles. However, prices decrease slightly for the 2022 model year.

- The manufacturer has a strong effect on used car prices.

---

## Conclusion

Several factors influence used car prices. Buyers should compare vehicles based on manufacturing year, mileage, condition, manufacturer, transmission, vehicle size, and vehicle type instead of relying on price alone.
